ABOUT US

Our Mission


is to make the planet little sweeter by creating celebratory moments with each sweet we serve.

Matchia Cupcake


We carefully bake in small batches by hand each day using the finest ingredients sourced from local, sustainable, and whenever possible, organic producers. We are proud to offer cupcakes and cakes that are freshly baked within hours, if not minutes, for your enjoyment

Our Bakery


our bakeries are designed and built to green certification standards, we operate a delivery fleet of low emission vehicles, we clean using biodegradable cleaning products, and we use recyclable and compostable boxes, utensils, cups, napkins and bags.
